
Prediction of Alzheimer's Disease Conversion from Mild Cognitive Impairment Using Serum miRNA Expression Profiles

Dataset:
- GSE150693
- 197 human samples
- 83 MCI converters
- 114 MCI non-converters
- 2562 miRNA biomarkers

Methods:
- Data preprocessing
- Standardization
- Random Forest Classification
- PCA Visualization
- Feature Importance Analysis

Results:
- Accuracy: 62.5%
- Top 10 candidate miRNA biomarkers identified
- PCA showed overlap between classes

Conclusion:
Machine learning using serum miRNA expression profiles can predict Alzheimer's disease conversion better than random chance.
